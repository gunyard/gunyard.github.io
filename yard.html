Deeper Purpose

GUNYARD is a vision - a community arisen to protect and practice American Liberty. In its original, Revolutionary sense, Liberty lives in the freedom to speak, to think, to arm, and to defend. With creators who value innovation, responsibility, and the right to engage on their own terms, expression can be open while protected.

GUNYARD can be a workshop for liberty, a place to explore a balance of rights, respect, and responsibility. We would foster the free exchange of ideas by way of collaboration and refinement in an open yet structured venue. We could incorporate the most valued elements from emerging digital social life without depriving each other of our basic vocabulary or our common sense, AND while incorporating the tools that share, collaborate, and build. NOTHING exists today that embodies the vision of a singular venue for brainstorming, sharing, developing, demonstrating, and living liberty - until GUNYARD.
